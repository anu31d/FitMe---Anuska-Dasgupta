
## 🎯 Overview

**VigorAI** is a premium, AI-powered fitness companion that delivers personalized workout experiences through Google's Gemini AI. Designed with a stunning dark-mode interface featuring neon accents, it combines cutting-edge technology with an intuitive user experience to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Whether you're training at home or in the gym, VigorAI adapts to your lifestyle, energy levels, and personal conditions to provide safe, effective, and motivating workout guidance.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Personalization
- **Smart Onboarding** — Collects your fitness level, goals, health conditions, and preferences
- **Adaptive Workouts** — Generates exercises tailored for gym or home environments
- **Contextual Motivation** — Delivers empathetic, personalized encouragement based on your daily check-ins

### 💬 Intelligent Chat Assistant
- **Natural Conversations** — Chat with VigorAI for workout advice, form tips, and motivation
- **Health-Aware Responses** — Safely acknowledges conditions like PCOS and hypertension
- **Clean Formatting** — Responses are elegantly formatted without markdown clutter

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- **Daily Check-ins** — Track your energy, mood, and stress levels
- **Streak System** — Stay motivated with continuous workout streaks
- **Visual Analytics** — Beautiful charts powered by Recharts

### 🎨 Premium UI/UX
- **Glass Morphism Design** — Elegant frosted glass components
- **Smooth Animations** — Powered by Framer Motion
- **Responsive Layout** — Seamless experience across all devices
- **Neon Accent Theme** — Striking green and purple gradient aesthetics

VigorAI is designed with safety in mind. It:
- Acknowledges health conditions (PCOS, Hypertension, etc.)
- Avoids recommending potentially harmful exercises
- Provides modifications based on fitness level
- Encourages users to consult healthcare providers
